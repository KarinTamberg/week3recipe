Delicious Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the tomato sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and mix it into the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
